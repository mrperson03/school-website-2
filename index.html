Campus Life

School Library

Was refurbished in 2022

Here the students can take out books and read, as well as do some fun activities with the school librarian. We go here once or twice a week and keep returning and exchanging books for new ones.



School Gym

Was built over 50 years ago

We go here to get some physical activity and we get to play games, like dodgeball, soccer, basketball, and others. We also go here when we have an assembly, and the whole school comes to the gym to watch.
